What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency secured by cryptography
and operating on decentralized networks.

What is a blockchain?

A blockchain is a distributed ledger that records transactions
across a network of computers.

What is a cryptocurrency wallet?

A cryptocurrency wallet is a digital tool for storing, sending,
and receiving cryptocurrencies.

How do I start investing in cryptocurrency?

Choose a reliable exchange, create an account, complete
verification, deposit funds, and buy cryptocurrencies.

How do I keep my cryptocurrency secure?

Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and
store your crypto in reputable wallets.

What are the most popular cryptocurrencies?

Popular cryptocurrencies include Bitcoin, Ethereum, Binance
Coin, Cardano, and Solana.
